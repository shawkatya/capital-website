Who We Are

Quality Child care in Old ottawa south

Our cooperative status and principles, rooted in the centers beginnings, value the role and involvement of both parents and staff in the development and well-being of children.

 

All staff members are professionally trained, equally considered and valued, making our staff retention remarkable.

 

Meals and snacks are nutritious and fresh, prepared on location daily by a trained cook and adapted to allergies and vegetarian preferences.

 

Established in 1973, Capital Day Care was originally established by the patrons of Trinity Anglican Church as many families in the neighbourhood were becoming two parent working families. In 1975 we signed our first purchase of service agreement with the City of Ottawa and became a licensed not for profit parent cooperative day care center.

 

We are committed to providing the community of Old Ottawa South and surroundings areas with high quality childcare. We deliver our programs under the Ministry of Education and follow the Child Care Early Years Act (CCYA).

 

Why choose Us?

Our qualified staff are passionate about guiding children through their physical, social, cognitive and emotional development.
